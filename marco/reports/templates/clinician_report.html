Limitations
Pharmacogenomic testing may not detect all clinically relevant variants. Structural variants, copy number variations, and rare alleles may not be captured by whole-genome sequencing approaches. Results should be interpreted in the context of the patient's complete clinical picture, including concomitant medications, organ function, and other clinical factors. Novel or rare variants of uncertain significance are not reported.

Intended Audience
This report is intended for use by qualified healthcare professionals and researchers. It is not intended for direct patient distribution without appropriate clinical interpretation.
